# Privacy Policy

**Agriculture RMS – Farmers Record Management System**  
Department of Agriculture, Municipal Hall Compound, Claveria, Philippines

*Last updated: January 2025*

---

## 1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how the **Agriculture RMS** (Farmers Record Management System) collects, uses, and protects your personal information when you use this system operated by the Department of Agriculture (DA) in Claveria. We are committed to protecting your privacy and handling your data in accordance with applicable laws, including the Philippine Data Privacy Act of 2012 (Republic Act No. 10173).

---

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Account and Registration Data

When you register for an account, we collect:

- Name and email address
- Password (stored in encrypted form)
- Any other details you provide during registration

### 2.2 Enrollment and Farmer Profile Data

When you submit an enrollment or claim (e.g., farmer registration, livelihood data), we may collect and store:

- **Personal information:** Surname, first name, middle name, extension, sex, date of birth, place of birth, civil status, religion, education, government-issued ID details
- **Contact information:** Mobile number, landline number, emergency contact name and number
- **Address:** House/lot, street, barangay, municipality/city, province, region
- **Household information:** Household head, relationship, number of members
- **Livelihood and farming data:** Main livelihood, farming activities, crops, livestock, poultry, fishing or farmworker details, income ranges, and related information
- **Other identifiers:** RSBSA reference number, insurance registration status, and similar program-related data
- **Documents:** Photo and other uploaded files you provide

This data is used to maintain accurate farmer records, support agricultural programs, and improve service delivery.

### 2.3 Contact Form and Communications

When you use the contact form or communicate with us, we collect:

- Name, email address, subject, and message content

We use this to respond to your inquiries and to improve our services. Contact messages may be stored and shared internally with authorized DA Claveria personnel.

### 2.4 Usage and Technical Data

We may automatically collect:

- IP address, browser type, device information, and similar technical data
- Logs of access and actions within the system (e.g., for security and auditing)

---

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

- **Operate the system:** Create and manage your account, process enrollments, and maintain farmer and program records
- **Deliver services:** Support agricultural programs, advisories, and communications relevant to farmers and stakeholders
- **Communicate with you:** Respond to contact form submissions, send important notices about your account or the system
- **Improve the system:** Analyze usage (in aggregate where possible) to improve functionality and user experience
- **Comply with the law:** Fulfill legal, regulatory, or government reporting requirements
- **Ensure security:** Detect and prevent fraud, abuse, or unauthorized access

---

## 4. Who Has Access to Your Information

Access to your personal data is limited to:

- **Authorized DA Claveria personnel** who need the data to perform their duties (e.g., processing enrollments, managing programs)
- **System administrators** for technical operation, security, and support
- **Relevant government agencies or partners** only when required by law or official program implementation

We do not sell your personal information. We do not share it with third parties for marketing purposes.

---

## 5. Data Storage and Security

- Data is stored on secure servers with measures to protect against unauthorized access, loss, or alteration
- Access is restricted by role and need; we use authentication and access controls
- Sensitive data is handled in line with our internal security and data privacy guidelines

While we take reasonable steps to protect your information, no system or transmission over the internet can be guaranteed to be 100% secure.

---

## 6. Data Retention

- **Account and enrollment data:** Retained for as long as your account is active and as required for program administration, audit, and legal compliance
- **Contact form messages:** Retained for a period necessary to address inquiries and for record-keeping
- **Logs and technical data:** Retained in accordance with our retention and security policies

When data is no longer needed for these purposes, it may be anonymized or securely deleted in line with our retention schedule.

---

## 7. Your Rights

Under the Data Privacy Act and our internal policies, you may have the right to:

- **Know** what personal data we hold about you
- **Request access** to your personal data
- **Request correction** of inaccurate or incomplete data
- **Object** to certain processing, where the law allows
- **Request deletion or blocking** of your data in cases allowed by law

To exercise these rights or ask questions about your data, contact us using the details in **Section 9** below.

---

## 8. Cookies and Similar Technologies

The system may use cookies or similar technologies to maintain your session, remember preferences, and support security and functionality. You can adjust your browser settings to limit or block cookies, but some features of the system may not work properly if you do so.
